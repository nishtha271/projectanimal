About Us

Humane Indian Animal Foundation, dba Adopt-a-Pet.com, is a non-profit, tax-deductible 501(c)(3) adoption advertising charity. Our mission (and passion) is to help get homeless pets out of the shelters and into loving homes. Let us know what you think! Our Mission

To fight for the rights, value and welfare of animals. To promote responsible pet ownership and maintain a continuous campaign to educate the general public about their responsibilities towards the welfare of pet dogs and cats.

Our Vision

In a world in turmoil caused by war, economic pressures and environmental problems, it is easy to forget that we are not the only species living and surviving on this planet. Large scale interference by humans in the animal world and nature is causing immense suffering for animals and nature alike. Compassion for all living beings is the driving force to rescue and rehabilitate animals in distress. We hope that compassion expressed in action will create a new balance in the human-animal-nature relationship. This is in the interest of all living beings.
